Description

This is a delicious lasagna recipe that everyone will love!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the ground beef until no longer pink.
  3. Add the marinara sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  5. Layer the noodles, beef mixture, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes until bubbly and golden brown.
