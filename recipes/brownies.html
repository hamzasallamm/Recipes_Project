Description

This is a rich and fudgy brownies recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Mix the melted butter and sugar in a bowl until smooth.
  3. Add the eggs and vanilla, then stir everything together.
  4. Add the cocoa powder, flour, salt, and baking powder.
  5. Stir gently until the batter becomes thick and even.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  7. Pour the batter into a greased baking pan.
  8. Bake for about 20 to 25 minutes.
  9. Let the brownies cool before cutting them into squares.
